Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Short version: we store your footage only to generate your reels, then delete it. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. You can delete everything at any time.

01Who we are

PostUp ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered content creation platform for athlete-creators, operating at postupapp.co.

For questions, contact privacy@postupapp.co.

02What we collect

Information you give us directly:

  • Account information — your email address and name when you sign up
  • Video footage — clips you upload to generate reels
  • Session details — text describing your session, outfit, vibe, creator type
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Lemon Squeezy; we never see card details
  • Usage data — how many sessions you've used, which features you access

Collected automatically:

  • IP address and browser type, for security and abuse prevention
  • Pages visited and time spent, via standard server logs

03How we use it

  • To generate your reels and AI captions
  • To manage your subscription and account
  • To send transactional emails — receipts, reel-ready notifications
  • To improve the product using anonymised, aggregated usage patterns only
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

We do not use your footage to train AI models. We do not sell your data. We do not run advertising.

04Your video footage

Uploaded clips are stored solely to generate your reels. Files are automatically deleted within 2 hours of processing. Generated reels stay in your library until you delete them or close your account.

05Third-party services

  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing. Their privacy policy ↗
  • Supabase — auth and database, hosted in AWS Singapore. Their privacy policy ↗
  • Anthropic Claude — AI caption generation. Their privacy policy ↗
  • Railway — server hosting for video processing

06Data retention

  • Uploaded clips — deleted within 2 hours after processing
  • Generated reels — kept until deleted or account closed
  • Account data — kept until deletion is requested
  • Payment records — kept 7 years as required by Australian law

07Your rights

  • Access all personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format

Email privacy@postupapp.co — we respond within 30 days.

08Security

All data transmission uses HTTPS. Data is encrypted at rest. We use Supabase Row Level Security so users only access their own data.

09Cookies

Only essential cookies for authentication. No tracking, no advertising cookies.

10Children

PostUp isn't intended for users under 13. If a child has created an account, contact us and we'll delete it immediately.

11Changes

We'll email you at least 14 days before significant changes take effect.
