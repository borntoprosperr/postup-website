FAQ
Everything you need to know

Questions answered

Do I need to know how to edit video?
+
Not at all. PostUp handles everything. Upload as many raw clips as you have — no limit. PostUp AI picks the best moments, edits them with smooth transitions, matches trending audio to TikTok’s current algorithm, and delivers a finished reel. You never touch an editing app.
What quality footage do I need?
+
Phone footage works perfectly. The AI works with any standard video from an iPhone or Android — vertical or horizontal, indoors or outdoors, any lighting.
Can I use PostUp for any sport?
+
Yes. PostUp is built for basketball players but the content engine works for any sport — gym, football, combat sports, running, skating, tennis. You pick your creator type and everything adjusts.
What happens to my footage?
+
Your footage is stored securely and only used to generate your reels. We never share or use your content for anything else. Delete everything at any time from your account.
How does the trending audio work?
+
PostUp analyses your reel type, creator niche, and the current TikTok algorithm to match trending audio to your footage. The audio is synced to the cuts so it feels like a professionally edited video, not clips pasted together. Audio refreshes weekly so your reels always sound current.
Is there a limit on how many clips I can upload?
+
No limit. Upload your entire day's footage, a full training session, or a whole week of clips. PostUp AI scans everything, picks the highest-energy moments, and edits them down into a sharp reel. The more footage you give it, the better the output.
Can I cancel any time?
+
Yes, always. No contracts, no fees. If you cancel, you keep access until the end of your billing period and all downloaded reels are yours to keep.
